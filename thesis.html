Thesis

The goal of my thesis is to examine the effects of CO2 injection on seismic data in the Tensleep sandstone at Teapot Dome. This project was completed using public data from the Rocky Mountain Oil Field Testing Center(RMOTC). The ROMTC dataset includes 2D and 3D seismic, well, core and production data.
